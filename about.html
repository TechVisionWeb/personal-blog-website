About

Hey I'm Charlotte and welcome to my website. I am excited about sharing with you the latest hair and beauty tips, tricks, hacks and honest opinions about beauty standards or what I like to call "hair and beauty politics" in the black community.

My hair and beauty tips are from my prospective as being a proud natural hair champion that indulges in wearing makeup. I am natural in the sense I don't use perms on my hair and as an aside, I also use minimal heat on my hair. If you want to rock your natural hair and don't know where to start, then this website is a good start for your hair and beauty journey to begin.

Going through my own journey of embracing my natural hair, I continually challenge my own views about colourism, texturism and featurism. Its important to me that all black women feel beautiful and confident in their own skin; part of that journey is having difficult conversations about the beauty standards in the black community. I hope by accessing this website, you begin to understand some of the ups, downs and navigations of black women or have a better insight to your own struggles.

I want this website to be a portal for black people, the mother and fathers of black / mixed children and our allies to be better educated about hair care, beauty and the "isms" in the black community. Come on board with this journey of self discovery and positive change!
