Maintain healthy curls and coils with the LCO method

  • First wet your hair thoroughly and detangle gently with your fingers.
  • Use a dentagling brush to gently remove finer tangles.
  • Generously apply leave in conditioner, such as shea butter - preferably sulphate and mineral free - to keep in moisture and soften hair.
  • Wait the hair to dry out and become less damp for the next step.
  • Apply hair oil such as caster oil to keep moistured sealed in further.
  • And that its! Enjoy your newly conditioned curls.
